Amazing Adventures Featuring: X-MEN 5

Released date: Apr 10, 1980

Artist and writer: Jack Kirby & Stan Lee

Synopsis:Professor Xavier interrupts the X-Men's latest training session to inform them that he has mentally detected a new mutant nearby. The X-Men find the mutant known as the Blob working at a carnival. They invite the Blob into the school so that Professor Xavier can test his powers. Afterwards, Professor Xavier invites the Blob to join the X-Men, but he refuses. In an attempt to maintain the X-Men's secret identities, Professor Xavier tries to mind-wipe the Blob, but he is able to escape.

Appearing Main Characters: The Golden Age X-men, The Blob, Professor X aka Charles Xavior

Series: Golden Age X-Men
