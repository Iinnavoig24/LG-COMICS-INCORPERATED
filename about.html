Welcome to a digitalized collection of some of my most favourite comic books ever. Every single book included in this website holds a special place in my heart. My father Lorenzo started me on my collecting path and still collects with me almost 10 years later. We have a variety of different comics that range from different publishers, release years, artists, and characters.
